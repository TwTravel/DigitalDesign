Advanced Microcontrollers
Final Projects


The following projects were produced in the last month of ECE 5760. The students were given the responsibility of choosing their project, then designing and building it. In 2017 we started using Intel/Altera/Terasic Cyclone5 FPGA. Earlier projects were built using the Altera/Terasic CycloneII FPGA, educational board. See the assignment or a few ideas for projects for further description. The project numbers are for identification and represent no other ranking. Send comments or questions to Bruce Land, Electrical and Computer Engineering
