With this project we were able to achieve our initial goal of having a Paint Brush Application using which one can paint by just moving hands and fingers in air, without using any other controlling device  like a mouse or joystick.

Though this system is just a prototype and is far from being turned into a commercial product our intent was only to demonstrate the exciting possibility of having fluidic interfaces with the computers and we feel we were able to successfully achieve the objective set out for.
